A day for devs of any level

Databases, Front-end, Back-end, Services...

...words we all know will be defined within a context where the limits between technologies are blurred and the horizons of knowledge and experience are broadened.

This will be an event for all those techno-geeks that enjoy learning some of everything.

¿What the F*@$ is "Fullstack"?

"Neo, in the matrix of web development" is what usually comes to mind when we try to imagine a Fullstack developer.
